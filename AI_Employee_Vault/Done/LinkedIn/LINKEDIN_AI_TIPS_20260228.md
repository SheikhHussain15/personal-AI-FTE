🤖 5 AI Automation Tips to Boost Your Productivity

After building an AI Employee system that works 24/7, here are my top automation tips:

1️⃣ **Start Small, Scale Fast**
Don't try to automate everything at once. Pick one repetitive task and master it first.

2️⃣ **Human-in-the-Loop is Critical**
Always keep humans in the decision loop for sensitive actions. Automation amplifies humans, doesn't replace them.

3️⃣ **Log Everything**
Every action, every decision, every error. Logs are your best friend for debugging and improvement.

4️⃣ **Use Approval Workflows**
Set up automatic approvals for routine tasks, but require human review for anything unusual or high-value.

5️⃣ **Monitor & Iterate**
Automation isn't set-and-forget. Review performance weekly and refine your rules.
