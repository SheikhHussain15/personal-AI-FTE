### 📌 What is Prompt Engineering?

**Prompt Engineering** is the practice of designing and optimizing inputs (prompts) to get the best possible outputs from AI models.

Think of it as learning to speak the language of AI.

Just like talking to a human:
- ❌ Vague instructions → Confused responses
- ✅ Clear instructions → Quality results

---

### 🎯 Why Does It Matter?

**The numbers don't lie:**
- 73% of companies are adopting AI in 2026
- AI users report 40% productivity gains
- BUT: 90% of users get suboptimal results

**The difference?** Prompt Engineering.

A well-crafted prompt can:
- ✅ Save hours of back-and-forth
- ✅ Unlock advanced AI capabilities
- ✅ Transform generic responses into expert-level insights
- ✅ Automate complex workflows
